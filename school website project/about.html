About us


Alaqsa Academy is an Islamic-based educational institution dedicated to nurturing young minds with knowledge, character, and faith. We offer classes from Nursery to Class 2, focusing on a balanced curriculum that integrates modern education with Islamic values.

At Alaqsa Academy, children grow in a caring and disciplined environment where they learn foundational subjects alongside Quranic teachings, duas, and moral development. Our aim is to build strong academic skills while instilling love for Islam, respect for elders, and good manners from an early age.

We aim to raise confident, respectful, and knowledgeable young children who are proud of their identity as Muslims and prepared for higher education and life ahead. Join us in giving your child the best start—academically, spiritually, and socially—at Alaqsa Academy..
